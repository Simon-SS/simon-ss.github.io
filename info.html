Why does this website exist?

Working with heavy JavaScript frameworks like AngularJS, React, and Vue in the past, I often look back at the early 2000s internet with heavy nostalgia and remember the simplicity. My goal with this website is to simplify information about my life while displaying my skills in computer science and Web design along the way. It is heavily inspired by Geocities websites of the mid-2000s that I would explore for any knowledge I could find that would help a 5 year old level up in World of Warcraft.
